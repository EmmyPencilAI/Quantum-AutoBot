# ⚛️ Quantum Finance

**The Future of Autonomous Trading is Now.**

Quantum Finance is an AI-powered decentralized trading platform built on Web3 infrastructure. It combines high-frequency AI decision-making with real-time execution across crypto markets, delivering aggressive, adaptive, and intelligent trading strategies.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Quantum started as an **EAS (Expert Advisor System)** in traditional forex markets — designed for precision and consistency.

As markets evolved, Quantum transitioned into **Crypto/Web3**, unlocking:

* Faster execution speeds
* Greater liquidity access
* Decentralized asset control
* Autonomous smart contract integration

Today, Quantum operates as a **fully on-chain + AI-driven trading system** with near real-time responsiveness and adaptive strategy switching.

```env

CONTRACT_ADDRESS=
TREASURY_ADDRESS=
```
